STOCKBET

How To Play

  1. Set the parameters of the game: your wallet size (the amount each person has to invest), and your holding period (the time you would wait to see results of your trade, in minutes).
  2. Research stocks by entering ticker names. For now, only stocks on the XXX exchange are available. Once you enter a valid ticker, the text area below will display recent news related to the ticker.
  3. Enter the ticker of the stock you want to purchase, and click Enter.
  4. The second player will repeat steps 2 and 3 above.
  5. Once both players have entered their stock pickcs, you will be directed to the Results page, where you can skim through investment literature.
  6. When your holding period has lapsed, the results section of the Results page will display the result of the betting game.

Limitations and Other Notes:

  1. For this release of the game, only stocks on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange will be available for lookup.
  2. The market must be open. Otherwise, there will be no movement in prices and hence no winner. Please see here for market open hours: hkex.com.hk/eng/market/sec_tradinfo/tradcal/tradcal_1.htm
