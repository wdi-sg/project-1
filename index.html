My Space Invader

Instructions

My Space Invader Game is designed for players who likes to play a never ending game where you rack up points until the player dies. The key to the game is to survive as long as possible by avoiding the enemies while getting points by shooting enemies with the player's laser the longer you survive the harder the game gets. It's game over if one of the enemy collides with the player. Try to get a higher score each time you play theres nothing better than beating your own highscore and setting a new one.

Player Controls :
Move Player = Arrow Keys (←↑↓→)
Fire Laser = Spacebar

: Player : Laser : Enemy
