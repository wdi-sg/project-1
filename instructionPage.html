Instructions

  1. Player Moves by one grid by any direction.
  2. Each move the player makes, the mummy moves towards the player by 2 moves towards the player.
  3. The Mummy will always try to make a horizontal move before attempting a vertical Move
  4. Try to trick the Mummy into moving to a blocked spot, so that you can make your move towards the exit.
  5. Once the player gets to the exit, the play is completed, Player proceeds to the next level.
