Instructions:
Your goal is to beat the dealer's hand value without exceeding 21 points.

If the dealer goes over 21 points, then any player who didn't already bust will win. If the dealer does not bust, then the higher point total between the player and dealer will win.
An Ace can count as 11 or 1 point, while jack, queen and king count as 10 points.

Click on green box to draw a card, and the red box to end your turn.
